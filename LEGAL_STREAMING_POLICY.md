# StreamNova Legal Streaming Policy

StreamNova may only embed or link media that is:

- owned by the site operator;
- licensed for web distribution;
- public domain or released under an open licence;
- an official studio trailer, clip, or preview that permits embedding; or
- linked through an official provider or rights-holder page.

Commercial feature films are not mirrored or scraped from unlicensed third-party streaming servers. When StreamNova does not have rights to a full feature, the player uses an official trailer or preview and links visitors to the rights-holder's official page.

Administrators are responsible for confirming distribution rights before entering direct MP4, WebM, OGG, or HLS URLs.
